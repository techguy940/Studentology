Trigonometry: Sides


sin: opposite / hypotenuse
cos: adjacent / hypotenuse
tan: opposite / adjacent
cosec: hypotenuse / opposite
sec: hypotenuse / adjacent
cot: adjacent / opposite

Trigonometry: Ratios


sin: 1 / cosec
cos: 1 / sec
tan: 1 / cot
cosec: 1 / sin
sec: 1 / cos
cot: 1 / tan
